# **DreamForge AI**

## **Problem Statement**

**The Last-Minute Life Saver**

## **Project Description**

DreamForge AI is an AI-powered productivity companion that helps users convert their goals into clear, actionable roadmaps. It intelligently breaks down complex objectives into manageable tasks, prioritizes them based on urgency, tracks milestones, and provides personalized AI guidance. By helping users stay organized and focused, DreamForge AI enables them to complete projects efficiently and avoid last-minute stress.
